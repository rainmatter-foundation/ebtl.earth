About US

EBTL is designed and managed by a ground team that lives on site with the support of Rainmatter foundation.  Our primary focus in the initial three years were developing a native tree nursery and also understanding the land with respect to water flows during monsoons. We have also intervened with mix of strategies like active tree planting, removal of invasives and natural regeneration of land.
